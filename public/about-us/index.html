Our Origin

HOW PEHCHANTV
CAME TO LIFE

In 2018, a small team of passionate Pakistanis living in Sydney noticed something missing — a media platform that truly understood them. Not just Urdu content broadcast from overseas, but stories told from within the diaspora, for the diaspora.

What began as a YouTube channel with a phone camera and borrowed microphone has grown into Australia's most trusted South Asian media platform — with tens of thousands of subscribers, original programming, and a professional events arm serving communities across the country.

Our name says it all. Pehchan — meaning identity, recognition, sense of self. Because we believe every immigrant's story deserves to be seen, heard, and celebrated.

Original Media Content
News, interviews, entertainment, and cultural programs created for the diaspora, by the diaspora.
Professional Event Management
From Eid galas to corporate multicultural forums — we craft unforgettable experiences.
Community Platform
A trusted space for announcements, community voices, and cultural conversations.

Mission & Values

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Everything we create is guided by values rooted in community, authenticity, and pride.

🎯
Authentic Storytelling

We tell real stories from real people. No filters, no pretence — just honest, culturally grounded content that resonates with lived experience.

🤝
Community First

Our community is not our audience — they are our co-creators. Every decision we make starts with: does this serve our people?

🌱
Cultural Pride

We celebrate South Asian heritage without apology. Language, food, faith, fashion — every thread of our culture is something to be proud of.

🔍
Journalistic Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of accuracy and fairness. Community media demands accountability — and we embrace it.

🚀
Empowering Voices

We actively create space for emerging South Asian journalists, filmmakers, and storytellers in Australia.

🌐
Building Bridges

We connect South Asian Australians with each other, with mainstream Australia, and with their homelands. Pehchan is a bridge — not a border.

"We exist so that every Pakistani, every South Asian, every immigrant living far from home can turn on their screen and see themselves — their language, their culture, their stories — reflected back with dignity and pride."

— The PEHCHAN TV Founding Team
JOURNEY
Our Journey

OUR MILESTONES

From a living-room camera setup to Australia's leading South Asian media platform.

2018
🎬 The Beginning
PehchanTV launches as a YouTube channel from Sydney. First video published on Pakistani community life in Australia. Equipment: one smartphone, one borrowed tripod.
2019
📈 First 1,000 Subscribers
Hit our first milestone. Began producing weekly content on immigration news, cultural stories, and community interviews.
2020
🌐 COVID Community Service
During lockdowns, PehchanTV became a lifeline — daily Urdu-language updates, mental health resources, and support information for isolated immigrants.
2021
🎪 First Live Event
Organised and live-streamed our first community Eid event in Sydney — 300+ attendees. The seed of our Events Management division.
2022
🏢 Events Division Launched
Officially launched PehchanTV Events as a standalone service for cultural festivals, corporate events, and community galas.
2023
🏆 10K & Awards
Crossed 10,000 subscribers. Recognised at the NSW Multicultural Media Awards. Expanded to Melbourne and Brisbane.
2024
🚀 50,000+ Going National
Surpassed 50,000 subscribers. Launched Hindi and Bangla content. 10+ major events across 4 cities. Full production team onboarded.
2026
🌟 The Next Chapter
New website, expanded programming, podcast series, and pan-diaspora partnerships across the US, UK, and Canada.
