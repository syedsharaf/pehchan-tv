Who We Are

BRIDGING CULTURES,
BUILDING COMMUNITY

PehchanTV was born from a simple belief: every immigrant deserves to see themselves reflected in media. We are a proudly Australian platform celebrating Pakistani, Indian, Bangladeshi, and South Asian communities — telling authentic stories, preserving cultural heritage, and creating belonging.

Original Media Content
News, interviews, entertainment, and cultural programs created for the diaspora, by the diaspora.
Professional Event Management
From Eid galas to corporate multicultural forums — we craft unforgettable experiences.
Community Platform
A trusted space for announcements, community voices, and cultural conversations.
